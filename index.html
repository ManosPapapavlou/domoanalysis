Professional Services

Verification & B2B Consulting Services

DomoAnalysis provides specialized consulting for engineering offices, contractors, and developers. We review, verify, and optimize third-party structural studies — ensuring technical accuracy, regulatory compliance, and cost efficiency.

Structural Verification & Peer Review

Independent checking of analyses, design documentation, and detailing. We ensure compliance with Eurocodes, ΚΑΝ.ΕΠΕ, and local regulations — identifying improvements in safety, economy, and constructability.

Optimization of Structural Studies

Analytical re-evaluation to minimize material use, improve load paths, and streamline reinforcement. We use OpenSees, SOFiSTiK, SAP2000, and MATLAB/Python automation to deliver transparent, practical optimizations.

B2B Collaboration & Technical Support

Long-term cooperation with engineering and architectural firms for project-specific consulting. Specialized support in non-linear analysis, seismic assessment, retrofitting concepts, and digital workflows (BIM, parametric).

These services are tailored for professionals seeking a reliable technical partner to review, strengthen, or expand their engineering capabilities.
